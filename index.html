Pokémon

In this page I will talk about the famous franchise and its' origins.

The creator

Satoshi Taijiri was born in the rural area of Machida, Japan. He liked to catch and collect bugs that were found around the area, which slowly began to dissapear due to urbanization works.

As a teenager, his interest for videogames grew, and as an adult, Satoshi decided to combine his love for bugs and videogames and create a videogame to catch and collect creatures. That is how the idea of Pokémon was born.

The game

The first two editions of the game were released in Japan in 1996 as "Pocket Monsters Aka" (Pokémon Red) and "Pocket Monsters Midori" (Pokémon Green). Designed for Nintendo's Gameboy, each version of the game included exclusive creatures, which could only be obtained in the other version by connecting two consoles (one with each version of the game) through the Game Link Cable and transferring a Pokémon from one to the other.

The game was a success in Japan, and was released in the United States in 1998 as "Pokémon Red" and "Pokémon Blue". By 2017, the game had sold over 31 million copies worldwide, earning its' first Guinness World Record as the Best-selling videogame (excluding bundle sales).
